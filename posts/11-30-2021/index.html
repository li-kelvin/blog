Uploaded November 30th, 2021

I always pictured my 20's to be different. More adventures. More challenges. More memories. Instead, I've been staring at the same screen for 10+ hours a day for the past year. The life I am living is boring and when you are bored, do something new! This is the beginning of my 10-minute journals, where I write about anything on my mind.

Entry #1:

Life has been beating my ass recently. It makes it harder to watch the people you love succeed. Damm, I sound like an asshole. I'm just scared of falling behind. Thanks to Mark Manson, I know that problems suck, they need to be solved, and I'm going to be the solution.

This is the tale of Kelvin Li, finally deciding to carry the boat. May this hopefully one day serve as a reminder that anything is possible as long as you do the work.
