I'm wasting my life

Uploaded November 30th, 2021

Ego is the enemy. And yet, I find myself face to face in the mirror resentful and regretful of not living up to what I can be. Isn't it interesting how my mind will warp itself into telling itself that it deserves to be great? I always pictured my 20's to be different. More travel, more adventures; instead, I stare at this screen for 10+ hours a day.

"Then find something to do." This is the tale of Kelvin Li, finally deciding to carry the f****** boat and make something out of his life. May this hopefully one day serve as a reminder that anything is possible as long as you do the work.
