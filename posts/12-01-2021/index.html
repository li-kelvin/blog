Time to start living.

Uploaded December 1st, 2021

A friend once told me that the point of life was simply to die.

Whats the point of living?

I don't know.

And maybe that's ok. Life isn't as simple as 10-year old me thought.

Life is a great big puzzle. A puzzle you solve with no clue of its final image. Occasionally a bird might shit on it, or the wind may blow some pieces away.

But its a nice puzzle.

So until then, keep living. Go through life with a smile, don't be an ass, and enjoy every second.

Cause you'll miss this shit.
