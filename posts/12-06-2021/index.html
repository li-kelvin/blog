Being more present

  • archive
  • about
Uploaded December 6th, 2021

Recently I caught myself "going through the motions" of life. I am having a much harder time focusing, especially on tasks that I am not fully committed to.

By only going through the motion, you allow the mind to wander. When the mind wanders, we don't feel as much discomfort. However, the mind is never improving either.

To become more present in life, I will implement two new techniques.

  1. Wake up and complete your morning routine as slow as possible. Move slow, but ensure every step has intent and purpose.
  2. Whenever needed, take five big breaths and organize your thoughts. Once collected, choose the first task and complete it.
